## Next Recommendations

- For client presentation, consider reducing the story to 1-2 primary scenarios per stage so the large-screen modal does not feel too dense.
- Customer Journey can keep multiple personas; Employee and Business Operation currently work more as team scenarios and can later add specific role personas such as marketing manager, supply-chain planner, and IT / AI enablement lead.
- AI opportunities can be further tagged as Quick Win, Scale-up, or Strategic Bet and connected to the current roadmap labels used by benchmark cases.
