# General Mills Three-Journey Case-Scenario Audit v3

Date: 2026-06-25

## Review Principles

- Customer Journey: keep cases tied to consumer touchpoints, brand/channel experience, purchase decisions and repurchase.
- Employee Journey: keep cases tied to employee enablement, knowledge work, frontline assistance, decision support, AI governance and training.
- Business Operation Journey: keep cases tied to data foundation, insight and forecasting, product innovation, supply chain, marketing growth, governance and scaling.
- Remove broad cross-journey matches so consumer-facing cases are not incorrectly placed into employee or business-operation stages.
