08 General Mills Store Implementation

How to make it real without rebuilding the partner stack.

1

Connect existing cameras

Use existing store CCTV first; add entrance, consultation exit and hot-zone cameras only where coverage is missing.

2

Map store zones

Define entrance, hero product, new-launch area, consultation desk, product handover and order desk as measurable zones.

3

Bind sales workflow

Connect recognition events to LMS/CRM tasks: reception reminder, consultation prompt, quote follow-up and return-visit alert.

4

Coach the sales team

Use Intelligent Audience to evaluate scripts, capture needs, detect lost reasons and feed best practices back into training.
