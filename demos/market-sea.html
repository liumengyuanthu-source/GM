Southeast Asia · Market Strategy

Market Strategy

🌏Southeast Asia · 东南亚 · SG / MY / TH / PH

A mobile-first, multi-market chat-commerce region

SEA is several markets at once, united by mobile-first shopping. Marketplaces (Shopee, Lazada) plus chat commerce (LINE in Thailand, WhatsApp elsewhere) carry the business — largely run by local partners — with official brand stores already live across the region.

Live: Lazada + Shopee (SG/MY/TH/PH) Chat: LINE (TH) + WhatsApp Sites: blackmores.com.sg / .co.th Shape: Multi-market, partner-run
01 · Market landscape

Market characteristics, audience & channels

One region, many markets — mobile-first, marketplace-heavy, chat-assisted.

🧭Market characteristics

  • Multi-market — SG, MY, TH, PH each differ in channel mix & language.
  • Mobile-first — shopping, chat and payment on the phone.
  • Marketplace-dominant — Shopee & Lazada are primary storefronts.
  • Chat commerce — LINE (Thailand) and WhatsApp for assisted selling.
  • Promo & livestream led — campaigns, affiliates, TikTok Shop rising.

👥Audience / persona to verify

  • Urban, mobile-first families — immunity, kids, everyday wellness.
  • Younger adults — beauty-from-within, energy, sleep.
  • Deal-seeking — responsive to campaigns, vouchers, livestream.
  • Comfortable buying via chat with a person (LINE / WhatsApp).
  • Trust imported, Australian-made quality.

🛒Mainstream channels

  • Marketplace: Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop to verify
  • Chat: LINE (Thailand), WhatsApp Business (catalog + pay links)
  • Offline: Watsons, Guardian, Boots (TH) to verify
  • Owned: blackmores.com.sg, blackmores.co.th (Blackmores Club)
  • Note: stores are largely partner / distributor-operated.

03 · Our point of view

Digitalisation strategy — channel + consumer

Enable the partners running marketplaces and chat across each market, with one platform localised per country — then layer conversational membership on top.

Short-term · the reality today

Channel enablement

Customer = Shopee / Lazada operators & distributors
  • Regional content + AI creatives, localised per market & language
  • Marketplace official-store operations support & campaign kits
  • Chat-commerce enablement: WhatsApp / LINE catalog + agent assist
  • Per-market compliance guardrails + sell-through dashboard
Long-term · the goal

Consumer direct (DTC)

Customer = the end consumer
  • Conversational membership on LINE / WhatsApp (Blackmores Club)
  • Loyalty + reminders + habit check-ins across markets
  • Light owned commerce where it makes sense (SG / TH)
  • One platform, localised — scale to new SEA markets fast
The data flywheel: marketplace sell-out + chat-commerce engagement → one unified CDP → AI feeds back localised content, recommendations and offers. SEA proves the "one platform, many markets" model — the same playbook that extends to new markets.
