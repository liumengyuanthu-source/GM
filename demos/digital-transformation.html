Approach

Transform the experience — channel and consumer.

Blackmores already runs on plenty of platforms. The job isn't to rebuild them — it's to find the best path on top of what exists to modernise the experience across four layers: the business, the consumer, the daily product use, and the partners who run the channels. This is the framework we use to get there.

01 · Four experience layers

BX · CX · UX · EX

Transformation isn't one experience — it's four, and they have to move together.

BX

Business experience

The brand & commercial outcome — first-party data, margin, compliance, and the channel-to-direct shift.

CX

Customer experience

How a shopper discovers, buys, and is cared for — across marketplace, pharmacy, chat and owned channels.

UX

Use experience

The daily product moment — taking supplements, tracking goals, the habit that earns engagement.

EX

Enablement experience

The partners and store teams running the channels — content, tools, training and data they need to perform.

02 · The balance

CX vs BX — value for the consumer, value for the business

Good transformation creates consumer value and business value at the same time. Optimise only one and it stalls.

Consumer value (CX/UX)

  • Trusted, compliant guidance — supportive, never a claim
  • A daily reason to engage (Health Score, plan, reminders)
  • Seamless across the channels they already use

Business value (BX/EX)

  • First-party, consented data feeding one CDP
  • Channels enabled to sell more, with brand & compliance intact
  • A path from channel-led today to direct over time

06 · Use case

An immunity shopper, transformed

The same person, before and after — showing all four layers moving together.

Before

Buys Fish Oil on a marketplace store run by a partner. Blackmores never sees the shopper, has no data, and the relationship ends at checkout.

After

The partner is enabled with compliant content and an incentive; with consent, the shopper becomes a first-party member, lands in a daily Health Companion, builds a streak, and re-orders on subscription — data flowing to one CDP that improves every next interaction.
