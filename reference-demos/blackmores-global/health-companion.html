Engagement Growth Proposal

From a transactional touchpoint to a daily health companion.

Blackmores' relationship with most consumers is low-frequency — a supplement is reordered every one to two months, and the app or membership is opened only around that purchase. People take their supplements daily, but they don't engage the brand daily. The opportunity: ride that existing daily habit and turn the companion into a reason to open every day — building first-party engagement the channel-led business can't.

North-star metricWeekly Active Health Members (WAHM)
01 · The question

What we investigated

“Can a supplement brand earn a daily place in someone's health routine — beyond the moment of purchase?”

02 · Four lenses before a recommendation

How we looked at it

🔍Companion teardown

Today's touchpoints are transactional — reorder, points, the occasional content push. Engagement is concentrated in low-frequency purchase moments, so the app sleeps between orders.

📊Category scan to verify

The apps that win retention in wellness do it on daily habit — tracking, streaks, gentle nudges and a recap to look back on — not on shopping.

👤Consumer behaviour

The daily habit already exists: people take their supplements at the same time each day. The brand simply isn't riding that cue — there's no light, daily reason to check in.

🌍Global playbook

Habit loops (cue → routine → reward), streaks, badges, a shareable monthly “Wrapped”, and chat-native reminders — proven mechanics for turning a low-frequency brand into a daily one.

03 · Diagnosis

Strong foundation — but engagement sits in low-frequency moments

Blackmores already has the assets — health assessment, Health Twin, loyalty, trusted PGC content. They're just wired to the purchase, not to the daily routine. The white space is the daily health habit.

✅What's strong

Health assessment & Health Twin, a 4-tier loyalty program, TGA-backed trust, and a deep library of compliant PGC content.

⬜The white space

No daily check-in, no streak, no health score, no monthly recap — nothing that earns an open on a non-purchase day.

04 · The plan

Prove the habit loop fast, then turn it into capability

A short-term quick-win loop to prove daily engagement, and a long-term build that turns engagement into first-party data and a direct relationship.

Quick win · prove the habit loop

The daily loop

Daily check-in→Health Score + streak→Monthly Wrapped
  • A 5-second daily check-in: “Took your supplements today?” → streak + a simple Health Score
  • Monthly Health Wrapped — a shareable recap (streak, goals progressed, bundles tried)
  • Gentle reminders delivered chat-native (WeChat / LINE / WhatsApp) — no new-app friction
  • Statistics & encouragement only — supportive, never a health claim
Long-term · turn engagement into capability

From habit to first-party

  • Health Twin personalises the plan; adaptive goals and content keep it fresh
  • Replenishment & loyalty ride the same daily surface
  • PGC community & expert content (no UGC) deepen trust
  • Every check-in is consented first-party data → CDP → fuels the direct relationship
05 · Metric & guardrails

What we steer by

The north star is Weekly Active Health Members — the weekly trigger is a weekly open tied to a health habit, not a purchase. Guardrails: explicit consent & privacy (PIPL / Privacy Act), supportive language only (no medical claims), PGC content, TGA / Australian-made trust.
