Cross-Entity Bridge · Channel → Brand

Two entities, one consented bridge to first-party

Today the channel — a marketplace store, pharmacy or distributor — owns the shopper relationship and the transaction data. Blackmores does not. The two are NOT merged. The only connection is this governed bridge: the channel serves and grows the shopper, the shopper's first-party readiness is scored, and — only with explicit consent and through a privacy/RBAC data wall — a single minimal signal crosses, where it becomes a brand-new member in Blackmores' own CDP.
