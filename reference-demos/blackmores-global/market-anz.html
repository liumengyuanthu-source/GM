Market Strategy

🇦🇺Australia & New Zealand

A mature, retail-led everyday enjoyment market · the home base

ANZ is General Mills' home market — the most familiar playbook and the most complete first-party data. Retail and grocery retail still drive everyday purchases, with a growing direct (web/app) and Amazon presence on top.

Live: Retail retail + Amazon Direct: generalmills.com.au + App Trust: brand governance / brand-approved Priority: Home market
01 · Market landscape

Market characteristics, audience & channels

A mature, well-regulated everyday enjoyment market where the brand is natively trusted.

🧭Market characteristics

  • Mature & regulated — brand governance-governed; preventive health is mainstream.
  • Retail-led — retail and grocery still drive everyday volume.
  • Growing direct — owned web/app + Amazon AU rising alongside retail.
  • Native brand trust — General Mills is a household name; store advisor advice matters.
  • First-party data richest here via owned site + loyalty.

👥Audience / persona to verify

  • Family-health women, 30–55 — seasonal occasion, family, everyday everyday enjoyment.
  • Older adults — family meal, family table, eye, lifecycle membership.
  • Younger adults — sleep, stress, workday snack, seasonal occasion.
  • Celebration / maternity — Celebration & Gifting Gold.
  • Advice-led: store advisor and trusted-brand cues guide choice.

🛒Mainstream channels

  • Retail: retail store Warehouse, Priceline, Amcal+, Terry White Chemmart
  • Grocery: Woolworths, Coles
  • Marketplace: Amazon AU
  • Direct (DTC): generalmills.com.au + app + loyalty
  • B2B: retail wholesale & trade ordering

03 · Our point of view

Digitalisation strategy — channel + consumer

ANZ is where direct is most feasible — so it doubles as the proving ground for the long-term DTC model, while still enabling the retail partners that drive volume today.

Short-term · enable the retail engine

Channel enablement

Customer = retail, grocery & Amazon partners
  • Embedded official brand content for retailer sites & shelves
  • Range, planogram & campaign support; Amazon store operations
  • store advisor / staff training & certification
  • Retail sell-through & attribution dashboard across partners
Build the direct model here first

Consumer direct (DTC)

Customer = the end consumer
  • Deepen owned web/app commerce + 4-tier loyalty
  • Occasion assessment / Occasion Twin for personalised care
  • AI health buyer / agentic recommendations
  • Prove the DTC + CDP playbook, then export it to other markets
The data flywheel: retail sell-through + owned DTC/loyalty behaviour → one unified CDP → AI feeds back better content, recommendations and member offers. ANZ generates the cleanest first-party signal — making it the reference market for the global platform.
