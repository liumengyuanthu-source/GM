Knowledge graph grounding — make Blackmores answerable by AI

Product facts

  • Format, dose, price, active ingredients, TGA listing and shelf-life facts are structured as citable data.
  • Each claim links back to an approved source, not loose marketing copy.

Partner coverage

  • Channel-partner availability, in-store samples, SLA and stock signals become part of the answer layer.
  • AI can recommend the next best store or appointment, not just a product.

Policy and offers

  • Offers, promotions, returns, consent and eligibility rules are normalized for RAG.
  • Answers stay accurate when prices, offers or availability change.
