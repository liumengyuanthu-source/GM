01 · Market landscape

Market characteristics, audience & channels

What makes the China health-supplement market different, who buys, and where they buy.

🧭Market characteristics

  • One of the world's largest imported-supplement markets; cross-border (CBEC) is the main route for foreign brands.
  • Trust in origin — Australian-made / TGA is a strong proof point against counterfeits.
  • Content-led discovery: shoppers research on RED (小红书) & Douyin before buying.
  • Super-app ecosystems: discovery → store → pay → service can close inside WeChat.
  • Strict rules: no medical claims, PGC only, bonded-warehouse import.

👥Audience / persona to verify

  • Urban middle-class women, 25–40 — immunity, pregnancy, beauty-from-within, family health.
  • Mums-to-be & maternity — Pregnancy & Breastfeeding Gold a hero SKU.
  • Silver / older adults — joint, heart, eye health for self & parents.
  • Value-aware but insist on genuine, original-import goods.
  • Convert via KOL/KOC seeding and livestream rooms.

🛒Mainstream channels

  • Marketplace: Tmall Global (天猫国际), JD Worldwide (京东国际), Douyin e-commerce, Pinduoduo to verify
  • Content / seeding: RED (小红书), Douyin, Channels livestream (视频号)
  • Private domain: WeChat OA + Mini-Program + WeCom (anti-counterfeit, members, content)
  • Offline: pharmacy chains, bonded-warehouse fulfilment to verify
  • Note: most platform stores are operated by channel partners / TP agencies, not Blackmores directly.
02 · Where Blackmores is today

Blackmores' business status in China

From a daigou-fuelled boom to a platform-led, partner-operated present.

2015–2018

Daigou & CBEC boom

China sales surged (A$7.5m → A$143m); at peak China was over a third of group operating profit.

2018–2020

Reset

Tighter daigou rules and bonded-warehouse taxes; growth cooled, brand pivoted toward direct platform sales.

Now

Platform-led, partner-run

Tmall Global + JD Worldwide flagship stores; rebuilding with fewer, tailored products. Owned by Kirin.

03 · Our point of view

Digitalisation strategy — channel + consumer

Two moves on one platform: enable the channels that run the business today, while building the brand's own consumer relationship for tomorrow.

Short-term · the reality today

Channel enablement

Customer = the partners running Tmall / JD / Douyin
  • Supply one source of brand PGC + AI-generated, localised content & creatives
  • Push compliant copy guardrails (no claims / no competitors) and TGA proof
  • Livestream & campaign asset kits for the agencies' calendars
  • Sell-through & stock dashboard + attribution across platform stores
  • Training & certification for store operators
Long-term · the goal

Consumer direct (DTC)

Customer = the end consumer
  • WeChat Mini-Program membership store + WeCom private-domain SCRM
  • Health assessment / Health Twin onboarding to personalise care
  • 4-tier loyalty that turns CBEC traffic into first-party members
  • AI health buyer / agentic recommendations inside the journey
  • Pull the customer relationship back from channel to brand, step by step
The data flywheel: platform sell-out + private-domain behaviour → one unified CDP → AI analyses → feeds back better content, recommendations and member offers. Short-term we collect channel & sell-through data; that becomes the first-party base for the long-term DTC play.
